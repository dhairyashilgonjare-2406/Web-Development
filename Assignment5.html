Smriti Mandhana

Smriti Mandhana is an Indian international cricketer and one of the top opening batters in women’s cricket. She is widely known for her elegant left-handed batting style and consistent performances at the international level.

Basic Information

  • Full Name: Smriti Mandhana
  • Date of Birth: 28 July 1996
  • Role: Opening Batter
  • Batting Style: Left-handed
  • Recognition: Award-winning international cricketer

Career Highlights

Mandhana has represented India in all formats of cricket and has played important roles in international tournaments. Her performances have earned her global recognition and she continues to inspire young athletes through her dedication and skill. Instagram
