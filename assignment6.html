FIFA World Cup 2026

It will also feature 48 teams for the first time instead of 32, giving more countries a chance to participate. The tournament is expected to bring together millions of fans from around the world and showcase advanced stadium technology, cultural diversity, and unforgettable football moments. This historic event will mark a new era in international football.
